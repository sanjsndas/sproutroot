About SproutRoot Care

SproutRoot Care was born from the union of nature's serenity and human creativity. The name combines "Sprout" with "Mere" (old English for peaceful water), embodying our philosophy of creating spaces where plants and water elements coexist in perfect harmony.

Founded in 2010 by master gardener Evelyn Waters and landscape architect Marcus Greenfield, our team has grown to include over 50 certified professionals specializing in various aspects of luxury garden design and maintenance. Our work has been featured in prestigious publications including Architectural Digest, Garden Design Magazine, and The New York Times Home section.

What sets us apart is our holistic approach to garden design. We don't just create beautiful spaces - we create living ecosystems that evolve gracefully over time. Our designs incorporate principles of permaculture, sustainable water management, and native plant conservation while delivering the refined aesthetic our clients expect.

Gardening Tips

Expert advice to help you maintain a thriving garden.

Watering Wisely

Water your plants early in the morning or late in the evening to minimize evaporation.

Soil Health

Regularly test your soil to ensure it has the right pH and nutrient levels for optimal plant growth.

Seasonal Planting

Choose plants that are suited for your climate and the current season for the best results.

Mulching Benefits

Apply mulch around your plants to retain moisture, suppress weeds, and improve soil health.

Companion Planting

Plant compatible species together to enhance growth and deter pests naturally.

Pruning Techniques

Regularly prune your plants to promote healthy growth and improve air circulation.
